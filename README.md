# GRC Portfolio — ICU IT Environment Assessment

**Role Simulated:** GRC Analyst / IT Auditor  
**Framework:** NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) v1.1  
**Organization:** Northview Regional Medical Center (Fictional Scenario)  
**Assessment Period:** Q1 2026  

---

## Project Overview

This project simulates a full Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) 
Assessment of the ICU IT environment at a 200-bed regional hospital. 
It was completed as part of my transition into cybersecurity GRC from 
a 10+ year background in marketing and growth strategy.

The assessment covers all eight required sections of a professional 
GRC engagement, mapped to the NIST CSF v1.1 framework.

---

## What's Included

| Deliverable | Description |
|---|---|
| Full Assessment Report | 8-section written report including Risk Register, NIST CSF Mapping, Control Evaluation, Compliance, Maturity Assessment, and Remediation Roadmap |
| Executive Presentation | 12-slide PowerPoint deck for CISO/board-level audience |

---

## Key Findings

- **Overall Risk Posture:** HIGH RISK
- **Maturity Rating:** Level 1 — Ad Hoc (1.2 / 5.0)
- **14 risks identified** — 11 rated High
- **0 of 14 controls** rated Effective
- **7 of 10 HIPAA Security Rule standards** rated Non-Compliant

## Skills Demonstrated

- NIST CSF framework application

- Control effectiveness evaluation (administrative, technical, physical)
- HIPAA Security Rule compliance mapping
- Remediation roadmap development
- Executive-level risk communication
